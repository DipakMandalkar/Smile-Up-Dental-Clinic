

# SmileCare Dental Clinic Website

A professional, mobile-responsive dental clinic website with a white and blue medical theme, optimized for local SEO in Pune, India.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation & Home Page

### Design System
- White and blue color palette with medical trust feel
- Clean, minimal typography with proper heading hierarchy (H1–H3)
- Consistent spacing, rounded cards, and subtle shadows

### Home Page
- **Hero section** with a large background image area, clinic name "SmileCare Dental Clinic", tagline "Advanced & Painless Dental Treatments", and a prominent "Book Appointment" CTA button
- **About preview** — Short intro paragraph about the clinic's mission and values
- **Services preview** — Grid of 5 service cards (Teeth Cleaning, Root Canal, Implants, Braces, Whitening) linking to their individual pages
- **Google Reviews / Testimonials** — Carousel or grid of patient testimonials with star ratings
- **Before & After Gallery** — Image comparison gallery showcasing results
- **Embedded Google Map** — Iframe showing clinic location in Pune
- **Footer** — Contact details, clinic hours, social media links, and privacy policy link

### Floating Buttons
- Sticky Call and WhatsApp buttons visible on all pages (bottom-right corner)

---

## Phase 2: About Doctor Page

- Professional photo placeholder
- Doctor name: Dr. [Name]
- Qualifications (BDS/MDS), years of experience, certifications listed in a clean card layout
- Mission statement section
- CTA to book an appointment

---

## Phase 3: Individual Service Pages (5 pages)

Each service (Teeth Cleaning, Root Canal, Dental Implants, Braces & Aligners, Teeth Whitening) gets its own page with:
- Procedure explanation with icon/illustration
- "Who needs it" section
- Benefits list
- Approximate price range
- FAQ accordion section
- "Book Now" call-to-action button

A Services index page will link to all 5 individual service pages.

---

## Phase 4: Appointment & Contact Pages

### Appointment Page
- Clean booking form with fields: Name, Phone, Email, Preferred Date (date picker)
- Client-side validation with success toast on submission (frontend only)
- WhatsApp booking button (opens WhatsApp with pre-filled message)
- Emergency contact number displayed prominently

### Contact Page
- Address (Pune, Maharashtra)
- Working hours table
- Phone number and email
- Embedded Google Map

---

## Phase 5: Extra Features & SEO

- **Privacy Policy page** — Standard GDPR-compliant privacy policy
- **FAQ page** — General dental FAQs
- **SEO optimization** — Meta titles/descriptions on all pages, proper heading structure, semantic HTML
- **Navigation** — Responsive header with mobile hamburger menu, linking all pages
- **Social media links** in footer (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube placeholders)
